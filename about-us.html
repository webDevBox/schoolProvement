Problem: Poor Teacher Attendance & School Culture Hurt Students

Countries across the world spend billions of dollars towards public education, and yet corruption in education, specifically teacher absenteeism, remains pervasive and limits students’ ability to receive a quality education. Many countries’ students find that their teachers are absent 15 - 27% of school days, and poor school culture makes students and good teachers not feel valued for their hard work. Every development organization agrees that more community monitoring combined with positive and negative incentives is the answer, yet no organization has created a scalable solution to address teacher absenteeism and other school community issues. That’s why we created SchoolProvement.

What We Do to Solve the Problem

SchoolProvement is a low-cost intervention that uses a mobile app to improve public schools through better teacher attendance, direct and easy school communications, highlighting best schools and teachers, and encouraging positive school habits. After a school district or principal purchases the program for a school, users (students, parents, and community members) can sign up to monitor their teachers’ attendance and provide feedback and ratings for schools to learn about and improve their performance. Teachers can use the app to encourage users to engage more in their education by sending messages about things such as homework help and reminders for upcoming events. Principals and school district admins can verify their teacher’s attendance while learning where to focus their resources from crowdsourced feedback. Equally as important, the application provides habit “missions” for users to engage in positive behaviors that are proven to improve school culture and education. In short,SchoolProvement helps schools improve holistically by creating better culture and habits from student and parents all the way up to district administrators. All users’ identities are anonymous so that no one can be punished for participating, and various safeguards ensure that every absence report and feedback are verified by multiple users, teachers, and principals before being included in school or teacher profiles. This makes sure that everyone’s voice is heard and trusted so that schools can begin to focus on improving their accountability and performance from all sides.

Our Impact

The original absence monitoring concept of SchoolProvement was piloted as Raise Your Text during the Fall 2018 semester in Pohnpei, Micronesia. Although there was some initial resistance to the program, schools and teachers warmed up to the idea of holding themselves accountable with most people strongly agreeing that the program should continue. In rural schools where teacher absenteeism is the most prevalent, the program was shown to reduce teachers’ afternoon absences by almost half from 18.6% to 10%.

OUR Vision & Goals

By the end of 2020, our goal is to have our mobile app available and operational in 100+ schools in countries such as India, Indonesia, Ghana, Guatemala, Ecuador, Pakistan, Bangladesh, and Mozambique where teacher absenteeism is a major issue. By the end of 2025, we plan to help get 10 million students better access to education through our services.
